How it works

  • Choose a market

    Tell us where you want to live and we'll connect you with our expert Strategist.

  • Meet your strategist

    Chat one-on-one with your Strategist and dig into exactly what you're looking for in a town.

  • Explore suburbia

    Your Strategist creates a list of “must-see” towns based on your lifestyle and preferences.

  • Tour the towns

    List in hand, tour one-on-one with partner real estate agents, and get a feel for what life's really like.

  • Find YOUR town (and home!)

    Once you've found the town, continue working with our partner agents to find your dream home.
